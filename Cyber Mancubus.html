Cyber Mancubus

First encounter: E2M2.

Very tough defense cause of armor, when destroyed he's normal mancubus.

Shoots acid shots that deals high splash damage, be wary of that.


Weakness: Blood Punch: Blood Punch will deal massive damage and destroy its armor.

General Tip: The Arm Cannons have been reinforced and cannot be destroyed by damage.

General Tip: A chest Glory Kill will trigger an explosion that damages nerby demons.




Codex Entry

Mechanized and heavily augmented, the Cyber Mancubus is equipped for frontline warfare. Equipped with dual-barrel arm-mounted
mortar cannons, the Mancubus's integrated weapon system utilizes the body's naturally occuring biotoxins, refining this corrosive, viscous
membrane into a toxo-plasmid ammunition feed. Syphoned from the spinal gland and funneled intravenously to the weapon conversion system, the corrosive
secretion is discharged by an alternating firing mechanism, which dispenses the toxo-plasmid as either heated projectile or ignited fluid, the latter
resulting in a flamethrower-like incendiary spray capable of burning through armored plating.
