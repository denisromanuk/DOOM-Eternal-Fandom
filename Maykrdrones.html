Maykr Drones

First encounter: E3M3.

Very weak demon that drops ammo and health if killed with headshot.


Weak Point: Head: Headshot kills drop a lot of resources.

General Tip: Their thick hide mitigates damage.







Codex Entry

Urdak's population is mainly comprised of Drones, beings that exist as extensions of the Khan Maykr's will.
Though each is endowed with their own unique identity they remain biologically unable to resist a command from their Khan, and so
function in a perfectly synchronized colony system. Drones never leave Urdak, their bodies not yet mature enough to avoid harm while
crossing dimensions.
