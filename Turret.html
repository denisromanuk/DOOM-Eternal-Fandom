Turret

First encounter: UAC Atlantica.

Just a turret.


Weak Point: Eye: Shoot the Eye with the Precision Bolt and Ballista to deal massive damage.

General Tip: If you get too close, the Eye will duck and hide.

General Tip: If you aim lingers too long on the Eye, it will duck and hide.







Codex Entry

Invented by the Hell Priest Deag Nilox centuries ago, Turrets contain imprisoned lesser demons that serve as stationary guards for their dark masters.
Taking the form of a single unnatural eye and rising from their fellsteel containers to fire on any intruders nearby, Turrets defend the spaces around them and previously
served as a direct visual link f or their Hell Priest creator. With Deag Nilox now dead by the Slayer's hand the Turrets are directionless, and persist as undying traps that
lash out with bursts of energy at anything that comes near.
