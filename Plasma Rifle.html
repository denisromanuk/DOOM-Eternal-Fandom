Plasma Rifle

Obtained during E1M2 mission.

Very high long range high damage starting weapon.




Weapon Mods

Heat Blast: firing shots charges the three stages of a heat blast where each one is much more effective
Excelent for taking multiple enemies, great damage as well

Mastery:Power Surge: After triggering a fully charged Heat Blast, plasma projectiles will have a damage boost for a short time.

Microwave Beam: Plasma Rifle shoots long beam that instantly stun an enemy
can catch only one enemy and completely slows down character
great for interrupting enemy attacks

Mastery:Concussive Blast: Demon that are detonated by the Microwave Beam will trigger a concussive blast that falters nearby enemies.

Codex Entry

Designed by the UAC's military-tech division, the Plasma Rifle is the standart in energy-based weaponry. Firing a superheated
plasma in rapid-short burst intervals, the Plasma Rifle is capable of overloading energy-based shielding and liquifying enemy combatants.
