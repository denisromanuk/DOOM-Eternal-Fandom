Armored Baron

First encounter: The World Spear.

Cannot take damage when he has armor, after it's gone its just regular Baron.


Weak Point: Mace: Shoot the mace while it flashes green to detonate it and destroy the armor.

Weakness: Plasma Rifle: The Plasma Rifle can quickly destroy the armor. The Microwave Beam mod is most effective.

Weakness: Sentinel Hammer: Use a Sentinel Hammer slam when its armor is destroyed to daze it and extend its vulnerability window.







Codex Entry

The Armored Barons are grafted with the technology of Immora. Just outside the city gates they patrol with morning star gauntlets cursed
by the Dark Lord himself; one devastating strike obliterates both body and soul. Even the Maykrs fear these creations of demon and machine, for their
carnage is both relentless and unquenchable.
