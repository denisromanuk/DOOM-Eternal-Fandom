Heavy Cannon

Unlocked during E1M1 mission.

Great long range weapon for early missions.




Weapon Mods

Precision bolt: turns the rifle into a sniper, very high damage
great for taking out weakpoints

Matery: Headshot Blast: headshot kill will set off a powerfull, secondary blast that deals damage to nearby demons.

Micro Missiles: fires dozens of micro missiles that greatly falters enemies

Mastery: Botomless Missiles: Fire Micro missiles indefinitely without requiring a reload.

Codex Entry

A common complaint among users of this punch-packing conventional weapon is the weight
, as most of the component parts are forged in Tungstronium, a proprientary alloy developed to withstand
the enormous stresses imparted by the optional exploding dart ammunition. However, the addictional weight makes the Heavy Cannon
an ideal sniper rifle, as swaying and tremors are mitigated by the massive bulk. Trigger management is of the highest
priority when using this weapon, as self-inflicted gunshot wounds are common thanks to its no-pull reflex trigger system.
