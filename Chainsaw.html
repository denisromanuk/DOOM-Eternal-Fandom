Codex Entry

As the renowned weaponmasters of the Night Sentinels professed, it is the warrior that makes the weapon great. Albeit an unconventional choice, the chainsaw has become
a staple of the Doom Slayer's personal arsenal. For the kind of work that require enemy dismemberment, chainsaw is the right tool for the job.
