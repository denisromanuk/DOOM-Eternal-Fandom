Demonic Trooper

First encounter: Immora.

weakest enemy in-game, dies on one hit, drops ammo.


General Tip: They cannot be chainsawed or glory killed.




Codex Entry

Immora's warrior caste defends the ancient city, keeping watch over the walls since the betrayal of their creator millennia ago.
The imperial guard of Davoth remains an honored position amongst the citizens of Immora, wielding armor and weapons forged in the fires of hate and suffering.
Their loyalty and devotion to Davoth is unwavering, having waited eons for the return of their rightful leader.
