Cursed Prowler

First encounter: Reclaimed Earth.

If you'll get hit you'll lose the dash ability, Prowler can be killed by Blood Punch only at this point.


Weakness: Blood Punch: Once cursed, Blood Punching the Cursed Prowler is the only way to kill it a remove the curse.

General Tip: This demon's curse deals constant damage over time and disables your dash.

General Tip: You cannot target the Cursed Prowler with lock-on weapons while cursed.







Codex Entry

A binding curse of necrotic poison drips from the claws of the Cursed Prowler like venom. Known in Hell to be one of the most treacherous
forms of blood magic, only few have the teachings to conjure such sorcery Yet, Prowlers from the deepest and darkest hellpits have evolved to naturally
produce such power from their fingertips. One swipe curdles the blood of their victim and drains them of their essence with each heartbeat.
The only way to cease the curse is to cease the caster.
