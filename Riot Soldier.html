Riot Soldier

First encounter: Reclaimed Earth.

Has invincible shield, use splash damage.


General Tip: Its shield absorbs all frontal damage.

Weakness: Explosive: Detonate explosive weapons behind him to deal massive damage.




Codex Entry

Like their fallen comrades in arms, the once heavy infantry for Earth's defenses have now succumbed to the darkness.
Now marching under the banner of the Dark Lord, they unleash a firestorm upon humanity, sentinels, and any other thing within their warpath.
Emboldened by blessed shields raided from Urdak, the riot soldiers firmly assert their dominance within combat.
