Hell Knight

First encounter: E1M2.

Very agressive meele heavy demon.


Weakness: Chaingun: The Chaingun can quickly falter this demon.




Codex Entry

The Hell Knight, warrior beast among demons, has earned its place over the millennia in service of the eternal Archdemons.
As imposed by the sovereign houses, Hell Knights have long served as barbaric enforcers of the netherworld, impelling lesser demons by way of brutality.
Rare among demon-kind for its innate regard for primal orders of hierarchy, the Hell Knight exhibits adherence to the prevailing rule of its demonlord masters.
Requiring little compulsion on behalf of its highborne masters, Hell Knights readily serve the Elder Hell-gods for the extent of their lifespans, reaping great
satisfaction in the way of war and desecration.
