unlocked during E2M1 mission.

Has highest fire rate in game great for taking down heavy and superheavy demons cause of extra damage.




Weapon Mods

Mobile Turret: transform into four small turrets that massively increase fire rate
Quickly burns down big targets

Mastery: Ultimate Cooling: The Mobile Turret no longel stalls.

Energy Shield: creates a shield that blocks enemy shots for small amouth of time
Can also be used for taking weakpoints and demons with blood punch safely

Mastery: Shield Launch: Dealing enough damage with the Chaingun while the energy shield is active will launch it forward on release. Any demons hit by this projectile will falter.


Codex Entry

The M220 Chaingun is a 7.62x51mm AE six-barrel autocannon built for infantry deployment. Weighing over 80lbs, the M220 was conceived as a standart issue weapon
for ARC mech-soldiers fitted with exo-armor - mechanaized suits capable of supporting a heavier payload. The AE cartridge uses a special electromagnetic casing designed specifically
for the M220's rail system, which increases projectile velocity and reduces overheating.
