Cacodemon

First encounter: E1M1.

Heavy enemy and can deal high damage if not careful.


Weakness: Explosive: Fire a Sticky Bomb or Frag Grenade into its mouth
to trigger a Stagger.

Weakness: Ballista: The Ballista does bonus damage to flying demons.







Codex Entry

Hunters of the Sloughlands, the Cacodemon lurks the bottomless and murky depths of Hell in search of easy prey. An aimless wanderer, the Cacodemon is
mostly devoid of cognitive ability, sensory awareness, and otherwise commonly occurring impulses. Driven only by a singular desire to feed, the Cacodemon is likely
to appear wherever there is flesh to be consumed, bringing with it an insatiable propensity for hunger. It is said that the Cacodemon bears some resemblence to the cycloptic
titans of ancient Hell lore, leading Sentinel scholars to believe that the forgotten titans may yet live on in some disembodied form.
