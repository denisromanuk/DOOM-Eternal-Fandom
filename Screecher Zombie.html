Screecher Zombie

First encounter: The World Spear.

Buffs nearby demons if killed adding them more damage, health and speed.


General Tip: Killing the Screecher will cause it to temporarily buff all other demons in the arena.

General Tip: Demons buffed by the Screecher's screem are stronger, faster and harder to kill.

General Tip: Avoid killing the Screecher while more powerful demons are still alive.




Codex Entry

The Screechers were once human worshippers of the Ra'Itha Va'Tuum, an ancient Hell cult, but instead of being rewarded
for their devotion, they were tortured in the Citadel of Anguish for millennia. They became malformed by their frustrations of the
life-long deceit. Their blood-curdling screams of anger had fermented into something far more potent an intoxicating power desired by all demon-kind.
