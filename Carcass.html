Carcass

First encounter: E1M4.

Extremely annoying enemy cause of shield, blocks your movement and enemies before you.


Weakness: Plasma Rifle: The Plasma Rifle can overload Energy Shields and trigger an explosion.

Weakness: Blood Punch: Blood Punch can instantly destroy the Energy Shield.







Codex Entry

The product of inhuman bio-mechanical engineering, the Carcass was created in the remote labs of the Cultist enclave.
Neither truly living nor dead, it exists in a state of partial re-animation - its cybernetic armature simulating a living impulse within a
decaying organic host. Devised as a means of extending a soldier's usefulness in combat after death, the unliving host can only be destroyed by severing
the body from its cybernetic implants.
