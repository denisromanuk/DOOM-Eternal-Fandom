Stone Imp

First encounter: The World Spear.

Has much stronger defense than regular Imps.


Weakness: Shells: Stone Imps are vulnerable to the Full Auto mod on the Combat Shotgun.

Weakness: Sentinel Hammer: Stone Impswill die instantly from Sentinel Hammer slam.




Codex Entry

Imps that lost their lives on the battlefield are cast into the volcanic pits of the Burning Abyss. Bathed in Argent-befouled
basalt and slag, they emerge reborn with flesh of stone. The new hide of the Stone Imp is impenetrable to most known weapons; only rapid
multi-vibrations or the crushing force of a Sentinel Hammer can damage their rock-like exterior. In addition to their retained agility, Stone Imps can
channel the pits of their rebirth and self-immolate in Hellfire.
